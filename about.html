The City of Weavers

Rooted in deeply woven Indian heritage and modern sustainability.

Welcome to AK Enterprise, proudly operating from Panipat, Haryana—globally recognized as the "City of Weavers." Our city's legacy in textiles stretches back centuries, evolving from historic handloom craftsmanship to becoming Asia's largest textile recycling hub today.

We are a leading manufacturer and exporter specializing in premium home furnishings, rugs, hand-tufted carpets, and bath linens. Taking advantage of Panipat's immense infrastructure, which processes over 100,000 tonnes of recycled garments annually and accounts for nearly 75% of India's blanket production, we blend authentic Indian artistry with global market trends.

Industry Insights & Strengths

  • Circular Economy Leaders: Panipat pioneers the sustainable transformation of discarded fabrics into high-quality yarn, significantly reducing global textile waste.
  • Unmatched Production Scale: Access to a workforce of over 800,000 skilled artisans and modern MSMEs allowing for both bespoke luxury and high-volume output.
  • Export Excellence: Dedicated export channels reaching out to major global markets including the USA, Europe, Japan, and Australia.
