Welcome To AK Enterprise

A dedication, hard work and sincerity is major tools to grow.

Welcome to AK Enterprise, rooted in Panipat, the city of weavers. We specialize in the manufacturing and export of elegant & innovative designs in Home Textiles & Furnishing. Our curated collection includes premium quality rugs, handwoven carpets, bed linens, bath luxury, and exquisite handicraft decor.

Operating from the heart of Haryana, we blend traditional Indian craftsmanship with modern market trends. We are uncompromisingly committed to creating textile products that are elegant, durable, and contemporarily designed to elevate spaces globally.

Why Choose AK Enterprise?

  • Direct from Panipat: Sourcing the finest handlooms, carpets, and rugs directly from the legendary textile city.
  • Custom Manufacturing: Bespoke designs and private labeling for global luxury brands and hospitality.
